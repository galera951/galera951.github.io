Who are you, Gleb?

About me

My name is Gleb, and I'm a student of MIPT. I was born in Pskov and spent my entire childhood there. Before MIPT, I studied at the Pskov Technical Lyceum (PTL) and Kvantorium Pskov. There I met good people, many of whom we still communicate with. In addition, I graduated from a music school in the French horn class.

My interests

I am interested in physics and programming, and I am trying to combine these interests. Now in my free time I am learning web development, because I am interested in it. I also work in the project activity group of the Department of General Physics at MIPT.

Sometimes on weekends I travel around Moscow and its environs, and take some great photos. You can find some of them on my VK page or in the relevant section on this site. In my free time, I attend the basketball section at my institute.

My skills

As I wrote above, I understand something about physics and programming. For example, I know Python, the LaTeX markup language and have recently become interested in web development, so I will probably soon know HTML5, CSS and Javascript. In addition, I have basic skills in creating graphics in Adobe Illustrator, animation in Adobe After Effects (you can see my first project in Adobe AE here, and sorry for the bad music :D). Previously, for a long time I was engaged in 3D modeling at Autodesk Inventor, now at the institute I have a course on modeling in Solidworks.

My projects

You can see some of my projects on my GitHub. Maybe in the future I will open more repositories, and you will see more of my work.

My achievements

In the first semester of my studies (2021), my friend and I made a project that won the Contest of Questions for Choice from the Department of General Physics of MIPT. In the future, I plan to participate in various other contests and will keep you informed about my successes!
